Why use the Blockchain?

Using blockchain technology to make your purchases guarantees greater transparency on your consumption data and allows you to authenticate the transaction through the public register.

How to track transactions?

Transactions are recorded in a public register that can be consulted by anyone; it is this register that guarantees the validity of a blockchain.
